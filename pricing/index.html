Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Just contact us on WhatsApp and we'll adjust your plan. The difference is prorated based on your remaining time.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a money-back guarantee instead (7, 14, or 30 days depending on your plan). This way you get the full experience with all channels and features. If you're not happy, you get a full refund.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. The price you see is the price you pay. No activation fees, no equipment rental, no extra charges for 4K or international channels. Everything is included.
What happens when my plan expires?
Your access continues until the last day of your plan. There's no automatic renewal. When it expires, just message us to renew. No surprise charges.

Each plan includes 1 device connection. If you need more, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll set up a multi-device package at a special rate.
